Meet Our Faculty

Our team of experienced educators brings clinical expertise and academic excellence to the classroom

Qualifications & Experience

Our faculty members hold advanced degrees in nursing and related fields, with many years of clinical experience. They maintain current practice credentials and regularly participate in professional development to bring the latest healthcare knowledge to our students.

Teaching Philosophy

We believe in student-centered learning that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application. Our faculty are committed to mentoring each student individually, fostering critical thinking skills and clinical competence essential for nursing practice.

Research & Scholarship

Many of our faculty are actively engaged in nursing research, contributing to the advancement of healthcare knowledge. Students have opportunities to participate in research projects and evidence-based practice initiatives.
