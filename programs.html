Our Nursing Programs

Comprehensive healthcare education for aspiring nursing professionals

Degree Programs

We offer accredited nursing programs designed to prepare you for success in healthcare

BSN (Bachelor of Science in Nursing)

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: F.Sc. Pre-Medical with at least 50% marks

Our BSN program provides comprehensive nursing education with a strong foundation in theory, clinical practice, and research. Graduates are prepared for RN licensure and diverse nursing roles in various healthcare settings.

Apply Now

Post-RN BSN

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: Registered Nurse with valid PNC license

Designed for registered nurses seeking to advance their education, this program builds on existing knowledge and experience to prepare nurses for leadership roles in healthcare. Flexible scheduling options available for working professionals.

Apply Now

Nursing Assistant Program

Duration: 1 Year

Eligibility: Matriculation with Science

Our certificate program prepares students for entry-level positions in healthcare settings, providing essential skills in patient care under the supervision of registered nurses. Includes hands-on training and clinical experience.

Apply Now

Specialization Opportunities

Advanced learning paths for specialized nursing careers

Critical Care Nursing

Specialize in caring for patients with life-threatening conditions in intensive care units, emergency departments, and cardiac care units.

Pediatric Nursing

Focus on providing healthcare to children from infancy through adolescence in hospitals, clinics, and community settings.

Maternal Health Nursing

Specialize in caring for women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period, ensuring healthy outcomes for both mother and baby.

Admission Requirements

What you need to join our nursing programs

BSN Program

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical with minimum 50% marks
  • Matriculation with Science subjects
  • Age between 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Entrance exam and interview

Post-RN Program

  • Diploma in General Nursing
  • Valid PNC registration
  • Minimum one year clinical experience
  • Academic transcripts
  • Professional recommendations

Application Process

  • Complete application form
  • Submit required documents
  • Entrance examination
  • Personal interview
  • Acceptance notification
