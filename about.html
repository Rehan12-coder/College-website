About Ayan College of Nursing and Health Sciences

Excellence in Nursing Education Since 2010

Our Story

Established with a vision to transform healthcare education in the region

Our History

Founded in 2010, Ayan College of Nursing and Health Sciences has been at the forefront of nursing education, preparing competent healthcare professionals to serve communities with compassion and expertise. Over the years, we have grown to become a premier institution recognized for our commitment to excellence.

Our Achievements

We take pride in our 95% graduation rate and 90% NCLEX pass rate among our BSN graduates. Our college has been consistently ranked among the top nursing schools in the region, with accreditation from the Pakistan Nursing Council and recognition from the Higher Education Commission.

Our Future

We continue to expand our programs and facilities to meet the evolving needs of healthcare education. Our strategic plan includes developing new specializations, enhancing simulation technology, and strengthening our community partnerships to provide even better learning opportunities for our students.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional nursing education that prepares competent, compassionate, and ethical healthcare professionals dedicated to serving diverse communities with excellence and integrity.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of nursing education recognized for innovation, leadership, and excellence in preparing healthcare professionals who make a significant difference in global health outcomes.

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that shape our educational approach

Compassion

We believe in treating all individuals with empathy, dignity, and respect, fostering a caring environment for both learning and patient care.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards in education, research, and practice, continuously striving for improvement and innovation.

Collaboration

We value teamwork and partnerships with healthcare institutions, communities, and other educational organizations to enhance learning and service.
